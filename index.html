About us

As Timeless as the Game that Inspired It

Welcome to Pensey's Golf Club in California!

Golf, unlike any other game, respects its traditions and legends. Our club has united fascinating golf personalities — Snead, Hogan, Nicklaus, Palmer. Pensey's is not just a club, it's a history of the game itself, represented in a vibrant passionate community of golf lovers. We are happy to welcome professionals or beginners — Pensey's has a place for everyone.
